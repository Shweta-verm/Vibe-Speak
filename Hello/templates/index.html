About Me

VIBE SPEAK is using advanced technology to break down communication barriers for the Deaf and hard of hearing community. It employs machine learning and computer vision to translate sign language into text or speech, prioritizing user-friendliness and accessibility. Key features include real-time translation, a sign language dictionary, and learning modules, promoting inclusivity and sign language education for all.

My Work

Sign Language Detection

This system is to recognize and interpret sign language gestures. It translates these gestures into text , breaking down communication barriers and promoting inclusivity.

Sign to Speech

It facilitates communication by translating sign language gestures into spoken words, bridging the gap between deaf individuals who use sign language and those who do not understand it.

Games

The Sign Language Games project is all about creating fun and educational games that use sign language. These games help people learn and practice sign language in an enjoyable way. The project aims to promote understanding and inclusivity by making sign language more accessible and engaging for everyone.
