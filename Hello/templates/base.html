About Me

VIBE SPEAK is using advanced technology to break down communication barriers for the Deaf and hard of hearing community. It employs machine learning and computer vision to translate sign language into text or speech, prioritizing user-friendliness and accessibility. Key features include real-time translation, a sign language dictionary, and learning modules, promoting inclusivity and sign language education for all.
